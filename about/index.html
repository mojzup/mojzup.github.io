About Us

TututuOrchestra is a dynamic collective of musicians based in the Netherlands, which draws its inspiration from traditions such as jazz, impressionism and folk music of the world. The specialty of the orchestra is the use of compositional fragments as a driving force for guided improvisation. With the desire to combine orchestral sound with the fluidity of a small combo, the interplay between the orchestra and the conductress comes to the front, at times full of theatrical expressionism.
